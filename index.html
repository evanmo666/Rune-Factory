What is Rune Factory?

Rune Factory is a beloved series that masterfully blends farming simulation with action RPG elements, creating a unique gaming experience that has captivated players worldwide. Set in enchanting fantasy worlds, the series invites you to step into the shoes of a mysterious protagonist who arrives in a new town with no memory of their past.

What sets Rune Factory apart from traditional farming games is its seamless integration of combat, exploration, and relationship-building. You'll find yourself tending to crops in the morning, delving into monster-filled dungeons in the afternoon, and sharing heartfelt conversations with townspeople in the evening. This dynamic gameplay loop ensures that every day brings new adventures and discoveries.

The series is renowned for its charming art style, memorable characters, and the freedom it gives players to live their fantasy life exactly as they choose. Whether you prefer to focus on becoming the town's most successful farmer, a legendary monster hunter, or the heart of the community's social scene, Rune Factory accommodates every playstyle.

Begin Your Adventure

Your journey in Rune Factory begins the moment you step foot in your new town. As a mysterious stranger with no memory of your past, you'll be welcomed by the kind-hearted townspeople who offer you a chance to start fresh. Armed with basic farming tools and an adventurous spirit, you'll transform a small plot of land into a thriving farm while uncovering the mysteries of your identity.

Each day presents new opportunities: plant and harvest seasonal crops, explore dangerous dungeons for rare materials, befriend the local monsters, and participate in town festivals. The choices you make will shape not only your character's growth but also the relationships you build and the story that unfolds around you.
